SDSU Marathon

About the Marathon

Running in US has been growing by leaps and bounds over the years and San Diego is one of the cities leading this change. San Diego boasts of having the largest runners community in the US and San Diego runners across different age categories have been winning at the major marathon events across the country. San Diego has been referred to as the fitness capital of US.

SDSU is all set for the 4th edition of its annual city marathon, which will take runners through important landmarks of the city. We believe this event will go a long way in promoting running in the city and encourage a fit and healthy lifestyle.

Registration Process

Registration is done online. Go to the signup page and fill the necessary information.

Key points:

  • Registrations are online-only
  • Registered participants should be at the venue 15 minutes early
  • Collect your runner number at the counter near library
  • Please take care of your personal belongings
  • Lockers are available
  • The marathon starts and ends at SDSU library
  • Volunteers will be along the way for assistance
